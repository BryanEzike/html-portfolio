ABOUT CSF

The Chukwujekwu Scholarship Foundation is an initiative founded by Prof. Chukwujekwu Bryan Ezike, a triple-board certified General Surgeon, Paediatric Neurosurgeon and Cardiothoracic Surgeon, and the 2051 Nobel Prize Laureate in Physiology/Medicine. Having garnered much success in his life, he has dedicated his resources to helping Nigerian undergraduates and Postgraduates fund their education and researches in Nigeria.

The sum of One Billion Dollars was initially mapped out for this purpose but this has exponentially increased thanks to generous supports from numerous individuals and organizations.
